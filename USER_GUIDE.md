## ğŸ¯ Usage Tips

*   **Green Border**: Keep your hands inside the **Green Border** shown on the screen. Movements outside this box might not be detected accurately.
*   **Lighting**: Good lighting is key! Avoid strong backlight (windows behind you).
*   **Distance**: Sit at a normal distance from your webcam (approx. 2-3 feet).
*   **Background**: A plain background helps the camera see your hands better.

## â“ Troubleshooting

*   **"No hands detected"**: Move your hands closer to the camera or improve lighting.
*   **"Cursor is shaking"**: Try to keep your hand steady. The app has built-in smoothing, but very fast movements can still be jittery.
*   **App not closing**: Click the **'X'** on the window or press **'Q'** on your keyboard to quit.
